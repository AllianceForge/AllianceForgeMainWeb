Directive 01: Core Principles

Forging a New Era

The Alliance Forge economy is engineered for longevity and resilience. We are building a self-sustaining digital nation, not a short-term speculative bubble. Our primary objective is to create a robust ecosystem that rewards meaningful participation and strategic, long-term investment.

A Revolution in Gaming: "Play-and-Own"

At its heart, Alliance Forge is a high-quality, engaging game. We have evolved beyond the flawed "Play-to-Earn" (P2E) model to pioneer a "Play-and-Own" (P&O) system. This approach prioritizes immersive gameplay while offering players true, verifiable ownership of their in-game assets as NFTs. This fosters a deep connection between players and the game world, aligning the success of both developers and the community through perpetual royalties from secondary market sales.

Directive 03: The Vesting Forge

The Vesting Contract is the critical bridge between in-game effort and real-world ownership, designed to reward long-term commitment.

  • The Process: Players irreversibly deposit their earned, soulbound $ALLIANCE into the Vesting Contract.
  • Linear Vesting: This deposit initiates a linear 28-day vesting schedule.
  • Daily Rewards: Each day, 1/28th of the corresponding $CYBER value becomes claimable by the player.
  • DAO Control: While the 28-day vesting period is the launch parameter, the Alliance Forge DAO will hold the authority to propose and vote on changes to this duration post-launch.

Directive 05: A Sustainable Economy - Value Creation and Stability

Our economy is engineered for the long term, supported by defined revenue streams and powerful token sinks.

Revenue Streams

  • Node License Sales: One-time fees (paid in USDC) for lifetime access to operate a game node.
  • Marketplace Fees: A 2.5% fee on all peer-to-peer asset transactions conducted in $CYBER.
  • Primary Sales: Revenue from first-party cosmetic items and event passes sold in $CYBER.

Economic Stabilizers

  • $CYBER Sink (Staking): Staking $CYBER removes it from active circulation. A 7-day unstaking cooldown period is implemented to prevent abrupt market volatility.
  • Decay Protocol ($ALLIANCE & $CYBER Sink): To create a powerful deflationary loop, any earned $ALLIANCE not deposited into the Vesting Contract within 45 days is automatically burned. For every $ALLIANCE token burned this way, a corresponding $CYBER token from the "Player Rewards" allocation is also permanently burned, directly linking player commitment to the scarcity of $CYBER.
  • The Treasury & Buy-Back Protocol: A portion of all ecosystem revenue flows into the Treasury. The Treasury will execute weekly market buy-backs of $CYBER and distribute it proportionally to all active stakers. This creates a direct feedback loop between ecosystem activity and token value, rewarding our most committed community members.

Directive 06: Your Path to Prosperity - How to Participate

Alliance Forge offers clear and rewarding paths for every type of participant.

The Asset Owner Path

  • Landowners: Owners of Land NFTs receive a passive yield of $CYBER from the "Staking Rewards" pool.
  • Node Operators: Operators of our game's infrastructure earn a steady stream of $CYBER from the "Node Operator Rewards" pool. Nodes are available in "Peace" and "War" configurations, with rarity tiers (Core, Mythic, Legendary, Epic, Rare, Uncommon, Common) that provide powerful in-game buffs.

The Active Player Path

  • Play and Earn Auron: Simply download the game and enjoy the traditional, off-chain economy without needing a wallet.
  • Connect and Earn $ALLIANCE: Connect a Web3 wallet to begin earning on-chain $ALLIANCE for your in-game achievements.
  • Vest for Value: Deposit your $ALLIANCE into the Vesting Forge to begin the 28-day conversion process into liquid $CYBER.
  • Own and Participate: Use your vested $CYBER to stake for revenue sharing, acquire valuable Land NFTs, or purchase a Node License through The Nexus, our dedicated Digital Asset Command Interface (DACI).
