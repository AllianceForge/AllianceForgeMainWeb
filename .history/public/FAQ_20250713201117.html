Frequently Asked Questions

What is Alliance Forge?

Alliance Forge is a strategic MMO that combines large-scale warfare with a player-driven economy built on blockchain technology. Players can engage in epic battles, conquer territories, and own in-game assets as NFTs.

How does the blockchain integration work?

Alliance Forge utilizes the Chronos Blockchain to provide true ownership of in-game assets (NFTs) and enable a player-driven economy. Our "Play-and-Own" model ensures that what you earn and acquire in-game is yours to keep, trade, or sell.

Is Alliance Forge free to play?

Yes, Alliance Forge is free to download and play. However, players can choose to connect a Web3 wallet to participate in the on-chain economy and gain true ownership of assets. Premium features and high-value assets are part of the Web3 experience.

What are $ALLFORGE tokens used for?

$ALLFORGE (or $ALLIANCE as per Forgenomics) tokens are soulbound tokens earned through gameplay. They represent in-game reputation and are used to be deposited into the Vesting Contract for conversion into $CYBER, our core asset token.

Can I play Alliance Forge on mobile?

Yes, Alliance Forge is designed to be cross-compatible and can be played on both PC and mobile devices, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of players.

How do I acquire Land NFTs?

Land NFTs can be acquired through the in-game Marketplace (The Nexus) using $CYBER tokens. Landowners receive passive $CYBER yields and can participate in territorial governance.

What are Node Operator Licenses?

Node Operator Licenses allow players to run a game node, contributing to the network's stability and earning $CYBER rewards. Nodes come in different rarity tiers, offering various in-game buffs.

What is the Vesting Forge?

The Vesting Forge is a smart contract where players deposit their earned $ALLIANCE tokens. These tokens then undergo a linear 28-day vesting period, after which they can be claimed as $CYBER tokens.
