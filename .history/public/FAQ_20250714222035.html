Alliance Forge: Gameplay & Blockchain FAQ

I. Gameplay

Q1: What is Alliance Forge?

A1: Alliance Forge is a strategic space-themed game where players build powerful cities, forge alliances, and battle for dominance across the planets of Aetheris and Sanctaris. You'll manage resources, develop advanced technologies, and command fleets in epic confrontations.

Q2: What is the main objective in Alliance Forge?

A2: The primary objective is to expand your influence, strengthen your city and forces, and collaborate with your alliance to conquer strategic objectives, defeat rivals, and ultimately become a dominant force in Sanctaris. Success is often measured by your strategic prowess, your alliance's power, and your achievements in various in-game events and leaderboards.

Q3: What kind of game is it? (e.g., RTS, MMO, 4X)

A3: Alliance Forge combines elements of strategy (like 4X games â€“ eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, eXterminate) with massively multiplayer online (MMO) features, allowing for persistent player interaction, alliances, and large-scale conflicts in a shared universe.

Q4: How do I start playing?

A4: Once the game is launched, you'll typically start by establishing your first city, learning the basic controls through an introductory guide, and beginning to gather resources and construct essential buildings. Joining an alliance early on is highly recommended!

Q5: What are Alliances and why are they important?

A5: Alliances are cooperative groups of players who work together for mutual benefit. They are crucial for defense, coordinating attacks, sharing resources and knowledge, participating in alliance-specific events (like Alliance Wars), and achieving larger strategic goals that would be impossible alone.

Q6: How do I build and develop my city?

A6: You'll construct and upgrade various buildings that unlock new units, technologies, resource production capabilities, and defensive structures. Strategic placement and upgrade priorities will be key to your success.

Q7: How does combat work?

A7: Combat involves deploying your fleets, comprised of various units with different strengths and weaknesses, against enemy players or AI-controlled entities. Strategy, unit composition, Commander skills (if applicable), and potentially alliance coordination will determine the outcome of battles. Expect to see battle reports detailing the engagement.

Q8: What are "Commanders" in the game?

A8: "Commanders" refers to you, the players! As a Commander, you are in charge of your city, your fleets, and your strategic decisions. In our community, verified players often hold the "@Commander" role, signifying their active participation.

Q9: How do I progress in the game?

A9: Progression involves upgrading your buildings, researching new technologies, expanding your territory, strengthening your military, completing missions or objectives, and actively participating in the game's economy and alliance activities. Your overall power and influence will grow as you achieve these milestones.

Q10: Will there be in-game events or tournaments?

A10: Yes, we plan to host various in-game events, challenges, and tournaments. These will provide opportunities for players to test their skills, earn unique rewards (including valuable digital assets), and gain recognition. Keep an eye on the #events-calendar and #esports-hub in our Discord.

II. Blockchain Integration & NFTs

Q11: How is blockchain technology used in Alliance Forge?

A11: Blockchain technology is used to provide players with true ownership of certain in-game assets in the form of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs). This allows for verifiable rarity, provenance, and the ability for players to freely manage or trade these assets on compatible marketplaces.

Q12: What are NFTs in Alliance Forge?

A12: NFTs in Alliance Forge are unique digital collectibles that represent specific in-game items, units, cosmetic enhancements, or potentially even land or strategic components. Each NFT is distinct and verifiably owned by the player holding it in their connected crypto wallet.

Q13: Do I need a cryptocurrency wallet to play?

A13: To fully interact with the blockchain features, such as owning, managing, or trading NFTs, you will need a compatible cryptocurrency wallet. However, core gameplay might be accessible without one, with options to link a wallet later to unlock full blockchain functionalities. More details will be provided closer to launch.

Q14: What are the benefits of having NFTs in the game?

A14: NFTs can offer various benefits, such as unlocking unique abilities or stats for units, providing exclusive access to certain game content or community channels (e.g., #legendary-chat for holders of Legendary NFTs), offering distinct cosmetic appearances, or representing verifiable achievements.

Q15: How do I get NFTs for Alliance Forge?

A15: NFTs may be acquired through various means, such as rewards for in-game achievements, participation in special events or giveaways, direct purchases during official sales, or by trading with other players on supported NFT marketplaces.

Q16: Can I trade my Alliance Forge NFTs?

A16: Yes, one of the core benefits of blockchain integration is that you will be able to trade your NFTs on compatible third-party NFT marketplaces, should you choose to do so. This empowers players with greater control and potential value realization from their in-game assets.

Q17: How is NFT rarity determined and what does it mean?

A17: NFTs in Alliance Forge will have different rarity tiers (e.g., Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Core). Rarity can affect an NFT's appearance, its in-game utility or power, its scarcity, and potentially its desirability on the open market.

Q18: How is the security of my blockchain assets handled?

A18: The security of your blockchain assets (NFTs) primarily relies on the security of your personal cryptocurrency wallet. Always use strong, unique passwords, enable two-factor authentication (2FA) where possible, and never share your private keys or seed phrases with anyone. Alliance Forge will utilize established blockchain protocols for asset management.

Q19: Will NFT ownership grant any governance rights?

A19: Yes, we plan to implement a governance system where holding certain NFTs or meeting specific in-game criteria may grant you voting rights on community proposals and the future direction of certain game aspects. Check out our #governance channels in Discord for more information as it becomes available.
