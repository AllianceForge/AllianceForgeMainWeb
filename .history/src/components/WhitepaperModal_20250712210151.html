1.0 The Problem & The Solution

The Problem

The current landscape is dominated by a flawed "Play-to-Earn" (P2E) model that often results in short-term, speculative bubbles rather than sustainable digital economies. These models lack longevity and fail to create a deep connection between players and the game world.

The Solution

Alliance Forge pioneers a "Play-and-Own" (P&O) system. This model prioritizes fun and engaging gameplay at its core while offering players true, verifiable ownership of their in-game assets as NFTs. Our primary goal is to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that rewards meaningful participation and long-term investment. This approach aligns developer and player success through perpetual royalties from secondary market sales, fostering a deep connection to the game world.

2.0 The Technology & Architecture

To ensure stability, the economy is built on a three-tiered asset system. The game itself is being built using Unreal Engine, with foundational NFT assets integrated on the Cronos blockchain.

Auron

The Pre-Blockchain Resource

A traditional, off-chain in-game currency for players without a connected Web3 wallet. It is earned via gameplay and used for starter-gear repairs, fast travel, and entry-level cosmetics. Auron will never be convertible to on-chain assets.

$ALLIANCE

The Soulbound Token

An on-chain resource for wallet-connected players, earned through daily bounties, PvP victories, and world events. It is uncapped and non-transferable (soulbound), representing a player's reputation and achievements. Its sole purpose is to be deposited into the Vesting Contract for conversion into $CYBER.

$CYBER

The Governance & Value-Accrual Token

The heart of the ecosystem, $CYBER is a fixed-supply, ERC-20 token. It represents a direct stake in the digital nation and is used for trading all high-value assets like Land NFTs and Command Node Licenses.

The Vesting Contract

This smart contract is the bridge between earning and owning. Players deposit their $ALLIANCE, which is an irreversible action. The contract then initiates a linear 28-day vesting schedule, where 1/28th of the corresponding $CYBER becomes claimable each day.

Economic Stabilizers & Revenue

Revenue Streams

Funded through one-time Node License Sales in USDC, a 2.5% fee on all $CYBER marketplace transactions, and primary sales of cosmetic items in $CYBER.

Staking ($CYBER Sink)

Removes $CYBER from circulation and includes a 7-day unstaking cooldown to prevent market instability.

Decay Protocol (Deflation)

Any $ALLIANCE not deposited into the Vesting Contract within 45 days is burned. For every $ALLIANCE burned, a corresponding $CYBER from "Player Rewards" is also burned.

Treasury & Buy-Back Protocol

A portion of all revenue flows to the Treasury. Weekly, the Treasury executes a market buy-back of $CYBER, distributing it to active stakers.
